Introduction

I am a graduate student (M.S. candidate) in Department of Electrical Engineering, KAIST.
I am highly motivated to bridge machine learning and real-world engineering problems, especially by introducing carefully devised algorithms to optimize complex systems.
To this end, I have worked on applying Bayesian methods for high-dimensional optimization to design complex devices such as OLEDs with free-form lens structures.
I also have strong background on (organic) electronics and material science, and have experiences in OLED fabrication.
